Jennifer Lopez

Jennifer Lynn Lopez-Affleck, known by her maiden name and also shortened to J.Lo, is an American actress, dancer and singer. In 1991, she began appearing as a Fly Girl dancer on the sketch comedy television series In Living Color,where she remained a regular until she decided to pursue an acting career in 1993


Jennifer Lopez's music career took off with the release of her debut single "If You Had My Love" in 1999. The song quickly became a chart-topping hit.
